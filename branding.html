Brand Assets

There are many traits about our brand that allow us to represent you! Our goal is to make our brand identity feel playful, experimental and welcoming to motivate the next generation of creatives to become innovative with drinks. Check out the main brand guidelines that make us jitter juice.

Color

Jitter Juice’s color palette, built around CMYK-inspired tones: cyan, magenta, yellow, and navy blue—was carefully chosen to reflect the brand’s bridge between physical and digital experiences. The bright, energetic cyan, magenta, and yellow evoke a sense of creativity and playfulness, capturing the brand’s lively personality in real world touchpoints like packaging, murals, and in store displays. Navy blue acts as a grounding relational color, providing contrast and stability while connecting the more vibrant hues, much like the way Jitter Juice connects its physical products to a broader lifestyle experience.

This palette also mirrors the transition between the physical and digital realms, aligning perfectly with the brand’s purpose. The CMYK foundation references the world of printing and tangible materials, while the bold, modern application of these colors translates seamlessly into digital channels such as social media, apps, and interactive content. By using a color system that feels equally at home in both spaces, Jitter Juice reinforces its identity as a brand that energizes consumers wherever they encounter it, making every interaction visually cohesive, playful, and distinctly memorable.

Typography

Jitter Juice’s typography was chosen to reflect both personality and functionality, starting with the primary type, New Kansas Bold. Its rounded, friendly letterforms perfectly complement the playful energy of the Jitter Bug mascot, creating a welcoming and approachable tone across all brand communications. The boldness ensures strong visibility and presence, whether on packaging, signage, or digital media, while the subtle roundness conveys warmth and accessibility, reinforcing the brand’s inviting and engaging nature.

The secondary type, Area Normal Black, serves as a clean, legible counterpoint to the expressive primary font. As a modern sans serif, it supports universal design principles, ensuring that all written content is easy to read across formats and audiences. By pairing New Kansas Bold with Area Normal Black, Jitter Juice balances character with clarity, combining visual personality with functional readability to maintain a consistent, professional, and approachable brand voice in both physical and digital contexts.

Mascot

Choosing a mascot for Jitter Juice was all about capturing the brand’s playful, energetic personality. The Jitter Bug a simple, charming character with expressive arms and legs embodies the lighthearted spirit behind the drinks. Its minimal shape makes it instantly recognizable, while its animated nature reflects the quick, feel-good boost customers experience with every sip.

The Jitter Bug also serves as a welcoming symbol for the brand. Its friendly features and approachable design create an inviting atmosphere that encourages customers to join in the fun. Because it isn’t overly detailed or complicated, it feels universal where anyone can relate to it, smile at it, and feel connected to the brand through it. This approachability transforms Jitter Juice from just another product into a personality customers can bond with.

Beyond visuals, the Jitter Bug strengthens engagement across all brand touchpoints. Whether it appears dancing on packaging, waving from social media posts, or guiding customers through recipes, the mascot adds consistency and charm. It helps Jitter Juice stand out in a crowded market by offering a character that’s fun, memorable, and full of movement perfect for a brand built on creativity, comfort, and energizing flavor.

In the end, the Jitter Bug serves as more than just a mascot, it becomes the heart of the brand’s personality. Its simple, energetic design creates a friendly experience that customers can instantly connect with. By embodying Jitter Juice’s playful spirit, the Jitter Bug helps the brand stay memorable, and engaging.

Mixed Media

Jitter Juice’s use of mixed media imagery reflects the energetic, eclectic nature of urban environments. By blending real life textures, street elements, and dynamic photography with its playful illustrative character, the brand mirrors the layered visuals found in city spaces. This combination captures the movement, grit, and vibrancy of everyday urban life, creating a visual identity that feels modern and culturally connected.

Pairing the Jitter Bug with mixed media imagery also deepens consumer engagement. Seeing the character interact with tangible environments perched on a building ledge, dancing across a crosswalk, or popping out of a coffee cup makes the mascot feel alive and part of the consumer’s world. This fusion helps bridge the gap between imagination and reality, giving customers a sense of authenticity while maintaining the brand’s playful personality.
